Who Should Attend

Designed for South Florida DBE/ACDBE Business Owners

This summit is designed specifically for South Florida DBE/ACDBE business owners who have not yet completed or submitted their reevaluation and need practical support developing their Personal Narrative (PN).

What to Expect

A Hands-On Working Environment

The Personal Narrative Readiness Summit provides a hands-on working environment for DBE/ACDBE business owners who have not yet structured their Personal Narrative or need guided, step-by-step development support.

Participants will work through how to identify and structure the qualifying event, clarify the impediment, articulate the resulting economic impact, and develop a clear, organized Personal Narrative draft.

Participants Leave With

  • A Personal Narrative draft
  • An action checklist
  • Clear next steps to continue refining their Personal Narrative for submission readiness
